1.  <span data-ttu-id="9c22f-104">Klicka på noden **Servicehantering** \> **Vanligt** \> **Serviceorder** \> **Serviceorder**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9c22f-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service orders** \> **Service orders**.</span></span>
