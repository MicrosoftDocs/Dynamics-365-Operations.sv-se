1.  <span data-ttu-id="13fdd-104">Klicka på **Servicehantering** \> **Vanligt** \> **Serviceabonnemang** \> **Alla serviceabonnemang**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="13fdd-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>
