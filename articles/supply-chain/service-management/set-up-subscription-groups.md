[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="10f0f-104">Klicka på **Servicehantering** \> **Inställning** \> **Serviceabonnemang** \> **Abonnemangsgrupper**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10f0f-104">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **Subscription groups**.</span></span>
