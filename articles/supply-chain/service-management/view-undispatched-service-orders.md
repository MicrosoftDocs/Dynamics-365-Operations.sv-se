[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="536e9-104">I det här avsnittet beskrivs hur du visar aktiviteter i en serviceorder som inte har en arbetare tilldelad från en servicegrupp.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="536e9-104">This topic describes how to view activities in a service order that don’t have a worker assigned to them from a dispatch team.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
