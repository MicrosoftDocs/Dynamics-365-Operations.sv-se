## <a name="unit-conversions-to-msdyn_unitofmeasureconversions"></a>Enhetskonverteringar till msdyn_unitofmeasureconversions

Den här mallen synkroniserar data mellan Finance and Operations-appar och Common Data Service.

Finance and Operations-fält | Mappningstyp | Övriga Dynamics 365-fält | Standardvärde
---|---|---|---
DENOMINATOR | = | msdyn_denominator | 
NUMERATOR | = | msdyn_numerator | 
FACTOR | = | msdyn_factor | 
INNEROFFSET | = | msdyn_inneroffset | 
OUTEROFFSET | = | msdyn_outeroffset | 
ROUNDING | >< | msdyn_rounding | 
TOUNITSYMBOL | = | msdyn_tounit.msdyn_symbol | 
FROMUNITSYMBOL | = | msdyn_fromunit.msdyn_symbol |
