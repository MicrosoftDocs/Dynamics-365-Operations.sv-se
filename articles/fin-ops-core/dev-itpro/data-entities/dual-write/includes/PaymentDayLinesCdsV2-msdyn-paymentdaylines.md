## <a name="payment-day-lines-cds-v2-to-msdyn_paymentdaylines"></a>Betalningsdagsrader CDS v2 till msdyn_paymentdaylines

Den här mallen synkroniserar data mellan Finance and Operations-appar och Common Data Service.

Finance and Operations-fält | Mappningstyp | Övriga Dynamics 365-fält | Standardvärde
---|---|---|---
NAME | = | msdyn_paymentday.msdyn_name | 
LINENUMBER | = | msdyn_linenumber | 
FREQUENCY | >< | msdyn_frequency | 
DAYOFWEEK | >< | msdyn_dayofweek | 
DAYOFMONTH | = | msdyn_dayofmonth |
